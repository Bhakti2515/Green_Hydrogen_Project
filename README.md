Design and Analysis of a Multi-port DC-DC Converter for Green Hydrogen Production

A MATLAB/Simulink-based power electronics project focused on the design and analysis of a multi-port DC-DC converter for renewable energy and green hydrogen production applications.

Features:
Multi-source energy integration
Solar and battery-based DC input
Stable DC output regulation
MATLAB theoretical analysis
Simulink waveform simulation

Tools & Technologies:
MATLAB
Simulink
Simscape Electrical

Applications:
Green Hydrogen Production
Renewable Energy Systems
Smart Power Management
DC Microgrid Applications
